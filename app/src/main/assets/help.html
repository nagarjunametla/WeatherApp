Weather App


1. At the starting of the screen you don't see any bookmarked locations.You need to tap on Add Location FAB option to bookmark the city.

2. You will be navigated to Mark a City Screen, As you can see the Pin on the map, Press and hold down the pin then the Pin will be activated then you can move the Pin to mark the certain location.

3. Pinned location City will be populated on the Field which is in the bottom section of the screen. Then press the 'Mark Location' option to bookmark the city.

4. Bookmarked City will be added in the list of the Cities Screen.You can see the 'Close' icon in the list item, If you want remove that bookmarked city you can press the 'Close' Icon.

5. Tap on the item to see the Today's weather information
